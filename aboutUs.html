About us

Meet our team and learn about our work

We are ESDW (Empowering Seniors in the Digital World), an organization whose goal is to engage and familiarize seniors with the digital world.

The founders of the organization are five young teenagers who are willing to help older adults and provide them with opportunities that open up new horizons.

Our names are Ioli Sakouti, Iris Thoma, Dimitra Skalioti, Anna Varvarigou and Iliana Androutsopoulou. We participated in the CodeLikeaGirl camp by Vodafone.

It inspired us a lot and gave us the drive and knowledge to create this website as motivation for both older people to learn about technology and stay connected to the world, and younger ones to help and bring themselves closer to these people.

Our goal is not only to help our seniors but also to strengthen the bond between different age groups.
