Want to Support Our Mission?

If you would like to support our effort to share essential tech knowledge with elderly people — and you have some basic technology skills (but most importantly, the passion to help) — we would love to hear from you! Just send us an email, and we will get in touch to let you know which days we might need your help. Together, we can visit seniors at their homes and assist them in navigating the digital world.
